<p><i>World Clock</i> is a Flutter app that displays the current time and weather for various cities. The app uses the Material You theme to provide a modern and customizable user experience.</p><p><br><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li>Displays the current time for selected cities.</li><li>Shows the current weather (temperature, weather conditions) for selected cities.</li><li>Customizable Material You theme.</li></ul>
